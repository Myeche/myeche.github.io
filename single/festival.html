Festival of The Moving Image

This project was a design of a two color poster for the Festival of The Moving Image, a short Movie Festival. This year theme was "Press Forward, Don't Rewind", to show that City college will overcome its difficulty and move on.

Role

Designer

Visual Strategy

For this poster I used the cinema countdwon to show the idea of pressing forward without having the possibility of going back. I wanted to give a vintage movie feel to the poster so I chose the yellow and blue color.

Client

City College Cinema Department (2015)

Challenges

For this project I had to do a lot of different iterations to finally finalize my concept. I went from a lot of different techniques like photogrpahy then illustration to finally dinf the right idea.

Tools

Illustrator, InDesign
