Children's Book Project

This project was a complete redesign of the non-profit Children’s Book Project’s website. The goal was to create a better UX and simpler design to attract more viewers to the website and encourage donations.
See full website
See documentation

Role

UX designer and Front-end Developer

Visual Strategy

For the website, our team decided to choose a kid friendly palette but without a childish feel since the website is targeted toward adults. We simplified the sitemap and added a lot of imagery to give a better sense of space and modernity to the new website.
