About Me

Designer at heart, world-traveller, food and cat lover, I am driven by curiosity, always looking for new knowledga to aquire. Problem solving is my passion and no challenge is too big for me. My design identity revolves around layout reduces to its minimum with simple color palette and soft geometry. My specialization resides in Production design but I also have a lot of experience in screen design.
