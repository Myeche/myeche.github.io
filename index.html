About Me

A designer at heart, world-traveller, food and cat lover, I am driven by curiosity, always looking for new knowledge to acquire. Problem solving is my passion and no challenge is too big for me. My design philosophy revolves around layout reduces to its minimum with simple color palette and soft geometry. I specialize in production design but I also haveextensive in screen design.
